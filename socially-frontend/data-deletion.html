Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your account and all associated personal data from social.ly, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@sociallyevents.com with the subject line "Delete My Data".
  2. Include your Facebook name and Facebook ID in the email so we can identify your account.
  3. We will delete your data from our systems within 30 days of receiving your request and will notify you once completed.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the email address above.
