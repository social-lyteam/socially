Privacy Policy - social.ly

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 3, 2025

At social.ly, your privacy is very important to us.

Information We Collect

- When you log in with Facebook, we collect your public Facebook profile information, including your name, email address, and Facebook ID.

- When you interact with events (such as marking yourself as "interested"), we may store that information.

How We Use Your Information

- To personalize your experience and show you which of your Facebook friends are also interested in the same events.

- To improve our services.

Sharing Your Information

- We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties.

- We only use your Facebook information within the context of the social.ly platform.

Data Security

- We take reasonable security measures to protect your information.

- However, no online platform can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Deleting Your Information

- You can request that we delete your account and any stored information at any time by contacting us at support@sociallyevents.com.

Changes to This Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sociallyevents.com.
